Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Cult of SOX ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

2. We Collect Minimal Information

We're a meme token, not a data company. We don't want your data. We want your memes and your chaotic energy.

We may collect:

  • Public blockchain data related to your wallet address (because it's public anyway)
  • Information you voluntarily provide in our community channels
  • Basic analytics about website usage (like everyone else)

3. How We Use Your Information

We might use the minimal information we collect to:

  • Improve our website and token ecosystem
  • Send you memes and updates if you asked for them
  • Prevent fraud and secure our platform
  • Comply with legal obligations (if we absolutely have to)

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We don't sell your data because:

  1. We don't collect much data to begin with
  2. We're too busy making memes to run a data brokerage
  3. That would be very un-cult-like behavior

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Third-Party Websites

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We have no control over these sites and are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

When you use our social media links, you're subject to those platforms' privacy policies.

6. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect what little information we have. But remember: no online transmission is ever completely secure.

Protect your wallet. Not your keys, not your crypto. Not our problem.

7. Policy for Children

We do not knowingly solicit information from or market to children under the age of 13. If you're under 13, please don't use our site or buy our token. Go do your homework.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last updated" date.

We might not notify you of changes because we're probably making memes instead. Check back occasionally.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may contact us through our community channels. No guarantees we'll respond - we're probably making memes.

10. The Meme Clause

This is a meme token. Nothing in this privacy policy should be taken too seriously, just like the token itself. We're here for fun, chaos, and maybe some financial gains if we're lucky.
